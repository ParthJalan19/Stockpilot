SaaS Enterprise Core Modules

Explore the full set of business inventory components built into StockPilot to streamline your supply chain and billing cycles.

Multi-Warehouse Control

Track stock counts across warehouses, stores, or retail lockers. Real-time transfer workflows with approval tracking prevent discrepancy leaks.

QR & Barcode Utilities

Generate standard Code-128 barcode SVGs and QR codes linking straight to inventory listings. Print layout templates optimized for label makers.

Sales Invoicing & Purchase Orders

Manage invoices, calculate discounts, configure regional taxes (GST/VAT/Sales Tax), reserve items on pending orders, and download printable PDFs.

Fine-Grained Permissions

Manage users through Role-Based Access Controls (RBAC). Configure user permissions checkboxes to restrict product deletions or data exporting.

Shortcuts & Command Palette

Access navigation commands using global `Ctrl + K` hotkeys. Search across items, vendors, and clients or execute quick commands like Notion.

Audit Logs & JSON Backups

Keep track of operational changes through complete before-and-after audit tables. Download full database JSON backups or restore tenant states instantly.
